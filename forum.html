3 hoteller i vandkanten

Her kan du bo på Seychellerne

På ethvert øhop på Seychellerne skal man bo godt. Derfor har vi besøgt de skønne tropeøer og håndplukket en række hoteller i vandkanten, der giver dig charme, atmosfære - og masser af værdi for pengene.

Carana Beach Hotel
- Luxury and pampering at Mahé

Carana Beach Hotel is a beautiful boutique hotel in Mahé, set in a crescent-shaped bay overlooking the sea.

Here you can relax and indulge in the senses with spa treatments, fantastic delicious food in the hotel restaurant, and sit back to the sound of the sea quiet gust.

You can also take a dip in the pool, have a drink at the bar, or take a stroll in the soft sand of the beach.

Spacious bungalow with sea views

You have a bright and spacious bungalow that is exclusively furnished and equipped with all the modern conveniences. From the bedroom you step directly onto the large wooden terrace where you can enjoy your private sea view.
